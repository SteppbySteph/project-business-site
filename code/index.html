Get zen everyday

1. Remove all that bad energy within yourself by shouting out loud whatever you feel like saying. Swear if you want to.
2. Start by saying three good things you have done recently. Try holding on to that positive feeling for 5 seconds and then repeat the process one more time.
3. Look yourself in the mirror and tell yourself; "I am beautiful no matter what they say. Words can't bring me down".
4. Go shopping using your partner's credit card. Tell him or her it's for grocery shopping.

You should get rid of things that don't spark joy to your home. This could be a Playstation 5, your partner or even some old clothes.

Firstly, put away your phone and stop scrolling your Instagram. Secondly use your time wisely and take a walk (or run if you want to challenge yourself a bit) and appreciate the beautiful nature that surrounds you. The fresh air will give you a break from things that might be overwhelming for you.
